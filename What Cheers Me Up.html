What Cheers Me Up

The idea of owning and managing a succesful company gives me joy. I'm not sure what type of company I'm going to create, but I have couple ideas in my mind. I have only one concern, and that is my company should be doing something good for the humanity, or for mother nature. I don't want to create a business just to make money. Most businesses fail because their only intention is making money, they don't have a soul. One must have faith in his business, but this faith is useless if your only purpose is making money. If I'm going to create a company, and start creating products , I must be sure, our products are helping somebody out there.
